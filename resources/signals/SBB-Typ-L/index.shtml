JMRI: SBB type L definition

This directory contains signaling definitions for the SBB (Swiss Federal Railways) from (with minor variations) about 1946. The definitions apply (again with minor variations) to all rail companies in Switzerland as they were mandated by the Federal Council (Bundesrat/Conseil Federal/Consiglio federale/Cussegl federal) and are part of Swiss law. References: Swiss railway signalling (English) Signale Typ L (German)

See the aspect page.

Signal Mast definitions:

  • Stop Aspect (Halt)
  • Aspect 1 (Fahrbegriff 1: freie Fahrt) Clear (maximum speed is defined in the route description)
  • Aspect 2 (Fahrbegriff 2) Maximum speed 40km/h
  • Aspect 3 (Fahrbegriff 3) Maximum speed 60km/h
  • Aspect 5 (Fahrbegriff 5) Maximum speed 90km/h
  • Aspect 6 (Fahrbegriff 6: Kurze Fahrt) Maximum speed 40km/h
Aspect 4 (Fahrbegriff 4) was discontinued in 1967, and is not documented further.
